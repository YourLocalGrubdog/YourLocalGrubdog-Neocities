This month's monthly summary is much shorter. To be brutally honest (while also keeping details minimal lol) this month was one of my worst ones in terms of dealing with depression symptoms. Nothing for read or listened so... yeah lol. 

So in no particular order! 

I did bust out the air dry clay again after years of not using it. I'm surprised that tub is still good. A little tough, but perfectly usable. I've been using it to make little trinkets so far, which I then paint over with acrylics (another medium I haven't touched in forever). It's been fun! My techniques certainly are not perfect, but I've enjoyed the little critters that now dot around my room. 

Speaking of doing stuff again; Pikmin 4 had an update that made me replay it. It was a simple thing, added difficulty options and a camera. It also added a new "collectible" in the form of decor pikmin you can find scattered around the map. And, of course, these tie into Bloom. Why wouldn't they? And yeah, mobile apps bad, yadda yadda, but this one tricks me into doing exercise. So the Pikmin 4 update getting me to play Bloom again is probably a good thing. I went on a whole two walks *outside* before the weather got too nasty for me to keep doing it. But I went outside!!! And did walks!!!!! And now I have more idiot carrot children to show for it :D I also got the 4th Anniversary Badge, which you got from walking 30k steps from the 22nd to the 30th. What it being on my profile doesn't tell you is that I grinded the last 14k steps in the last 4ish hours of the event. 

Yes, my body was on fire by the end. No, the badge does nothing. It is a goddamn PNG that sits on my profile that will vanish whenever the servers die. I love my idiot carrot children very much.
